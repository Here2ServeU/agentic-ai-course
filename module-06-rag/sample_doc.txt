ACME FINANCIAL — EMPLOYEE BENEFITS AND POLICY HANDBOOK (SAMPLE)

REMOTE WORK
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. Remote days must be agreed with
your manager in advance. Fully remote arrangements require Director approval and are
reviewed every six months.

HEALTH INSURANCE
The company offers three health insurance options: a Basic PPO plan, a Premium PPO
plan with lower deductibles, and a High-Deductible Health Plan (HDHP) paired with a
Health Savings Account. Employees select their plan during open enrollment each
November.

DENTAL AND VISION
Dental coverage is included with both PPO plans at no additional cost and covers two
cleanings per year plus basic procedures. Vision coverage is optional and can be added
for a small monthly premium.

WAITING PERIOD
New employees become eligible for health, dental, and vision benefits on the first day
of the month following 30 days of employment. There is no waiting period for the
401(k) retirement plan, which employees may join immediately.

PAID TIME OFF
Full-time employees accrue 15 days of paid time off in their first year, increasing to
20 days after three years of service. PTO does not roll over beyond a maximum of 5
unused days into the next calendar year.

EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT
Approved business expenses are reimbursed within 14 business days of submission through
the company expense portal. Receipts are required for any expense over $25.
